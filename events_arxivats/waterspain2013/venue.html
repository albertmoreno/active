VENUE

ZCAM is located in the "Río Ebro" campus (better known to taxi drivers as the Escuela de Ingenieros, or the Actur Rey Fernando bus stop) of the University of Zaragoza in the north of the city. The I+D (investigacion y Desarollo) building is at the Nothern end of the campus (i. e. further away from the city), it has red lateral walls and is located next a large parking for cars.
