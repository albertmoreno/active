ORGI 2015

Organic Electronics at ICMAB

23th-24th November

ICMAB-CSIC, Bellaterra, Barcelona

ORGI2015

This is an internal ICMAB workshop organized with the goal of bringing together the ICMAB groups working in different aspects of the field Organic Electronics to foster possible scientific collaborations and synergies among researchers.

The workshop will consist in the presentataion of the main research lines at ICMAB, short contributed talks, and a round table with senior experts from ICMAB.


The topics covered include:

  • Design and synthesis of organic molecules
  • Theory and modeling of organic semiconductors
  • Structural and Spectroscopic characterization
  • Nanopatterning and self-assembly
  • Processing of organic semiconductors
  • Fabrication and electrical characterization of organic devices


You are welcome to
