8th International Conference on Spectroscopic Ellipsometry

May 26th - 31st, 2019, Barcelona

The 8th International Conference on Spectroscopic Ellipsometry continues the well-established ICSE series to provide an excellent forum for scientists and engineers working in instrumentation, science and applications of spectroscopic ellipsometry and related techniques.

ICSE-8 will bring together international participants from many countries and offer them the opportunity to present their latest results. Both young and experienced researchers are kindly invited to participate and interact for dissemination, discussion, and advancement of science and technology topics represented in ICSE-8.

In addition, a day of Tutorials will be organized on Sunday, 26 of May, to provide an introductory session.

Following the high standards set by previous conferences, ICSE-8 will aim at presenting a useful balance of fundamental and applied research and industrial applications. Submission of original work is solicited on the topics suggested below and in related subject matters.

Suitable research results will be published in a special conference journal issue.

ICSE-8 will be held at the World Trade Center on Barcelona's downtown seafront.

Previous ICSE conferences

  • Berlin, Germany (ICSE-7, 2016)
  • Kyoto, Japan (ICSE-VI, 2013)
  • Albany, USA (ICSE-V, 2010)
  • Stockholm, Sweden (ICSE-4, 2007)
  • Vienna, Austria (ICSE-3 2003)
  • Charleston, USA (ICSE-2 1997)
  • Paris, France (ICSE-1 1993)

Scope

  • New Instrumental Developments
  • Imaging and Process Monitoring
  • Optical Modeling and Simulation
  • Anisotropic Materials
  • Mueller-Matrix Ellipsometry
  • Nanostructured and Metamaterials
  • Polarimetry and Scatterometry
  • Electronic Materials and Band Structure
  • Polymers, Organic and Hybrid Materials
  • Optical, Electronic and Energy Applications
  • Bio-related and Life Science Applications
