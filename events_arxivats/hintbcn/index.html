Scientific Workshop on
Biomedical, Health and Bio-Related
applications of Hybrid Materials

8th-9th June 2015

ICN2 | ICMAB-CSIC, Bellaterra, Barcelona

Scope

The scientific workshop “Biomedical, Health and Bio-Related applications of Hybrid Materials” is aimed to promote exchanges between chemists, physicists, biologists and engineers on the most recent achievements in hybrid materials with bio-applications. As a consequence, this COST MP1202 meeting is an optimal opportunity to reinforce complementary investigations of the design, control, and dynamics of hybrid organic-inorganic interfaces focusing on fundamental science and applied research in the field of medicine, environment and health.

Main topics will be:
  • Hybrid materials for Nanomedicine
  • Hybrid materials for environmental applications
  • Dynamics of interfaces
  • Interfaces in biological environments
  • Modelling and simulation of interaction processes
  • Characterization methods of interfaces
