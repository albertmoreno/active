Special Issue of the Journal of the European Ceramic Society

Elsevier publisher has agreed to our request to produce a special issue of the Journal of the European Ceramic Society  with contributions to ISNT2019. 

Best original contributions reviewed and selected by Guest Editors will be published. Contributions can be full length articles, short communications, state-of-the-art reviews, and feature articles.

Authors should use the regular submission procedure and then choose the option to select the special issue ISNT2019 in the menu ‘Choose article type’.
  • First submission date: 16th June 2019
  • Submission deadline: 1st October 2019
