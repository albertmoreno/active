 ISNT2019

10th International Symposium on Nitrides

Casa Convalescència, Barcelona, Spain
9-13 June  2019

We are glad to announce that the 10th International Symposium on Nitrides (ISNT2019) will be held in Barcelona, Spain from 9th to 13th of June of 2019

DOWNLOAD FLYER
PROGRAM
VENUE PLAN

The conference will take place in Casa de Convalescència. The Casa de Convalescència  forms part of the complex of the Hospital de la Santa Creu i Sant Pau, designed by the famous modernist architect Domènech i Muntaner (1852-1923).

The venue is located in the center of Barcelona at 5 minutes walk from the Sagrada Familia and very close to other famous modernist buildings in the city. The symposium will cover a series of main session topics concerning the research of nitride-based compounds such as:

 

 

  • Theory and modelling
  • Novel materials and synthetic approaches
  • Crystal chemistry
  • Thin films, sintering and metallurgy
  • Glasses and glass-ceramics
  • Silicon nitride and SiAlON ceramics
Chairs: Amparo Fuertes (ICMAB-CSIC) and Ralf Riedel (Technische Universität Darmstad)
