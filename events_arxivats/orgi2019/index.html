ORGI2019

2nd Organic Electronics Meeting at ICMAB

8-9 May 2019

INSTITUT DE CIÈNCIA DE MATERIALS DE BARCELONA
BELLATERRA, SPAIN
ORGI II is the second ICMAB workshop with the goal of sharing the last advances in the field of Organic and Molecular Electronics and fostering possible scientific collaborations among researchers from ICMAB and nearby groups. The workshop is organized within the Severo Ochoa project and will comprise invited and short contributed talks. The registration is free (with a limited number of places)
REGISTRATION CLOSED

Topics

  • Organic electronic devices
  • Physical phenomena at surfaces and interfaces
  • Single-molecule electronics
  • Molecular synthesis, processing and characterization
  • Organic materials for energy applications (photovoltaics, thermoelectrics)
  • Modeling and theoretical methods
