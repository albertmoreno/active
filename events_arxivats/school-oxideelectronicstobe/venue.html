Lodging & Venue

At a distance of only 1 km from Sant Feliu de Guixols, in a unique and quiet peninsula, is located the Hotel Eden Roc. The clear Mediterranean Sea surrounds along 360 meters the Eden Roc Resort.The climate is mild and pleasant. During the winter, temperatures rarely fall below 10 ° C. In summer, the fresh sea breeze will not allow the thermometer to exceed 30 ° C.The Eden Roc Resort consists of 3 separate buildings: Hotel Eden Roc Health Center Port Salvi Garden Eden 2 Restaurants: abundant barbecue buffet at noon and in the evening a buffet with elected specialities of our region. Comfortable lounges, elegant living rooms, 2 bars, own bridge and billiards room.For companies: congresses, seminars, presentations (4 air-conditioned rooms).The European Parliament celebrates its own congresses 5 times a year. Our experience guarantees a few days in a magnificent environment.

How to get there

  • TRAVEL BY CAR:

    Good comunications are given from nearly everywhere.

    TRAVEL BY PLANE:

    Daily flights from the main International airports to Barcelona (at 120 Km). Each 20 min. the Airportbus will drive towards the city centre. Aprox. each hour a bus (Sarfa) will leave from the North Station to Sant Feliu de Guíxols, it is about 2 hours way.

    TRAVEL BY TRAIN:

    The nearest train station is in Girona (at 30 Km.). A taxi from the train station to St Feliu costs approx. 46.- Euros
