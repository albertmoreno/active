MATBIO2017
The summer school “Materials for Biomedical Applications” is addressed to last year undergraduate, master and PhD students, who are interested in the development of materials with biomedical applications. The aim of the summer school is to present the design, development and application of new materials for a wide range of biomedical applications. The school includes lectures from international and local scientists, Hands on and management activities. As a consequence, this Scientific School is an optimal opportunity to discover, learn and practise on material science focusing on fundamental science and applied research in the field of biomedicine. The school is included in the Severo Ochoa activities.
