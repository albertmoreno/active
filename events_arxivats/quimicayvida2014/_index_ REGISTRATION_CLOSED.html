Venue

The workshop will take place in the Central Building of the University of Barcelona (Gran Via 555, 08007, Barcelona), a historical building in the center of the city, close to the worldwide known Plaça de Catalunya, the picturesque promenade Ramblas of Barcelona and the monumental promenade of Gracia, with the world famous buildings of Antoni Gaudí.

 

A complete set of hotels is available from young hostel to the highest class, just in the neighborhood of the workshop site. Please find attached the practical information about acommadation. We wait for your contribution and your presence in this unique workshop.

Speakers

The workshop includes the contribution of the partners of  both COST actions, XARMAE and international speakers who can help to get a clear overview of the current problems and working schemes to solve them.
