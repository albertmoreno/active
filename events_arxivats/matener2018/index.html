TOPICS

The Severo Ochoa summer school “Materials for Energy” is addressed to last year undergraduate, master and PhD students, who are interested in the development of materials with energy applications. The aim of the summer school is to present the design, development and application of new materials for a wide range of energy applications.


TOPICS:

  • Functional and advanced nanomaterials for a clean and sustainable energy production, conversion, storage and transport
  • Materials for energy conversion
    • Photovoltaics
    • Thermoelectric materials
  • Materials for energy storage
    • Next generation bateries
    • Supercapacitors
  • Materials for energy transport
    • Superconducting materials
  • Life Cycle Analysis of materials for energy
  • Advanced characterization techniques of energy materials

The school includes lectures from international and local scientists, hands on and management activities. As a consequence, this Scientific School is an optimal opportunity to discover, learn and practice on material science focusing on fundamental science and applied research in the field of energy. The school is included in the Severo Ochoa activities of the center.

REGISTRATION

Applicants must submit:

  • A CV, including contact details (maximum 2 pages)

  • A motivation letter (1 page) describing background, research interests and motivation for attending the school.

  • Include in the mail: your name, affiliation and contact email of one potential reference.

Send the complete information to matener@icmab.es

The term for applications is from 4 June to 6 July 2018
Successful applicants will be notified on 20 July 2018

The registration includes the school material, coffee breaks and lunches

Eligibility

The school will be limited to a maximum of 60 students, selected on the basis of their CV and motivation letter. Priority will be formasters-level students and early PhD researchers. However, advanced undergraduate students and advanced PhD researchers are welcome to apply as well.

We welcome applications from individuals worldwide with a variety of backgrounds, including materials science, physics, electronics, engineering, chemistry, biology and nanotechnology. The MATENER2018 Summer School is seeking for students with an excellent academic record and/or a strong commitment for scientific research of the candidate, as will be shown in the motivation letter.

Persons with disabilities are strongly encouraged to apply. There are no restrictions of citizenship or gender.

 

 

 

Posters and Flash Presentations

Participants are welcome to present a poster and a flash presentation (maximum 2 minutes) of their research project.


Please indicate your preference in your application mail:
• Poster
• Poster and Flash presentation (2 minutes)
• Only attendance

A maximum of 25 Posters and Flash presentation will be selected.
Awards will be given for the best posters and best flash presentations.
