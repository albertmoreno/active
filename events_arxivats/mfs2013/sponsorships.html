Product exhibitions and sponsorship opportunities

On behalf of the Spanish, Portuguese and Israel Societies of Microscopy, we have the pleasure to announce the Joint Meeting Microscopy at the Frontiers of Science 2013 that will be held in Tarragona, Spain, from the 17th to 20th September 2013 at the Palau de Congressos (http://www.palaucongrestgna.com/).

This meeting will cover all aspects of Microscopy applied to Life and Materials Sciences, as well as to other relevant fields in which Microscopy plays a pivotal role, that will be developed through a series of top quality plenary and invited lectures, as well as through high quality oral and poster contributions. In our view, this Joint meeting offers the opportunity to discuss and exchange the most recent experiences, as well as to strengthen scientific and technical collaborations. From these points of view this meeting provides an excellent forum to disseminate scientific and technical output and findings.

From the Companies’ point of view it is also a great chance to divulge new cutting edge research equipments amongst the Microscopy’s Community, achieve higher awareness of your Company and increase your network of researchers and scientists. Therefore we invite your Company to participate in this international congress by joining us as a Sponsor.

Three types of Sponsors will be acknowledged:

-        Main Sponsor – Companies sponsoring in the value of 4000 €.

Benefits:

-   One exhibition stand (3m x 3m) free of charge

-   The sponsor will be offered 2 attendee passes that entitles to all the benefits offered to the delegates, including coffee breaks, conference bag with program and book of abstracts, receptions, gala dinner, and social activities

-   A one-page advertisement will be added in the program and abstract booklet.

-   Sponsor’s advertisement/logo will be put on the conference internet home page with link to the Sponsor home page

-      The logo will appear at noticeable places during the conference

-      Company information may be placed free of charge inside the bag or conference pack.

-      Opportunity to offer a 15 minutes Technical Presentation during the Conference.

 

-        Regular Sponsor - Companies sponsoring in the value of 2000 €.

Benefits:

-   One exhibition stand (3m x 3m) free of charge

-   The sponsor will be offered 1 attendee pass that entitles to all the benefits offered to the delegates, including coffee breaks, conference bag with program and book of abstracts, receptions, gala dinner, and social activities.

-   The logo will appear at noticeable places during the conference, on the conference internet home page and in the book of abstracts.

 

-        Specific Sponsor - Companies subsidizing specific items and events during the meeting.

-        Conference Reception                                                                1000 €

-        Coffee Breaks                                                                             600 €

-        Drinks during Exhibition                                                           600 €

-        Conference Banquet                                                                   2000 €

-        Best Presentation/Poster Award                                                500 €

-        Microscopy & Art Awards                                                          1000 €

-        A Technical Presentation (15 minutes) during the Conference   500 €
