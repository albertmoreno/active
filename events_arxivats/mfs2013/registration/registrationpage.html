SCHOLARSHIPS:
The Spanish Microscopy Society (SME) offers scholarships for member students (or those becoming new SME members before registration to MFS2013). Scholarships will cover the registration fee for the students (150 €). Find more information to apply for the SME student scholarships here.
If you are still not a member of the SME, you can apply for the SME membership
The Portuguese Society for Microscopy (SPMicros) offers scholarships for selected Portuguese student participants. Scholarships will cover the registration fee for the students (150 €) and an additional support (100 €) for travel and living expenses. Find more information to apply for the SPMicros student scholarships here.
