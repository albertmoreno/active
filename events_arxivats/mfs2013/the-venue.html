How to get

The congress centre and the exhibition centre are in the centre of the city. The location of these two buildings make possible an easy access from the railway station (at 100 m), from the port passengers terminal (at 200 m) and from the Rambla Nova (at 100 m), the lattest, the commercial centre of the city.

 
TRANSPORT BY AIR
Tarragona is just 13 km away from the Reus Airport
and at 100 km. from the Barcelona Airport.
TRANSPORT BY ROAD
Tarragona has good bus services (EMT).
From the bus station, at few metres from the Rambla Nova, it is possible
to go to other coastal towns such as La Pineda, Cambrils, Vila-seca i Salou.
Excel·lent infraestructure of motorways:
A-16: Barcelona - Tarragona 
AP-7: València - Tarragona - Barcelona
A2: Madrid - Saragossa - Lleida - Tarragona
TRANSPORT BY TRAIN
Tarragona has good communications with other Spanish cities
such as Madrid, Valencia and Barcelona.
Route of the AVE:
Madrid - Camp de Tarragona Station (2,40 hours)
Route of the Euromed: 
Barcelona - Tarragona (53 minutes) 
València - Tarragona (2 hours)
