July 3-7, 2022, Barcelona, Spain

Thank you for coming to the 9th European Conference on Boron Chemistry!

The European Conferences on Boron Chemistry (EUROBORON) held every three years represent a forum where all aspects of boron chemistry are discussed by scientists, both professionals and students. 

Previous EUROBORON meetings were successfully held in 1997 at Platja d’Aro (Spain), in 2001 at Dinard (France), in 2004 at Pruhonice (Czech Republic), in 2007 at Bremen (Germany), in 2010 at Edinburgh (UK), in 2013 at Radziejowice (Poland), in 2016 at Suzdal, Russia and 2019 at Montpellier (France). This time EUROBORON comes back to Spain, where the conference series started. As the conference hosts we would like to welcome you all to be with us in Barcelone starting July 3rd, 2022.
