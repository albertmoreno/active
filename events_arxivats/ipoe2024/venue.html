Venue

Casa Convalescència

The conference is scheduled to take place at the Casa Convalescència, which is considered one of the last great works of Catalan Art Nouveau. It is a part of the Hospital de la Santa Creu i Sant Pau complex, which is a perfect example of the Catalan Modernista style, and has been recognised by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site.
