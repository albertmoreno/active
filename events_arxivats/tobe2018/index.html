MP1308 TO-BE COST Action ("Towards Oxide-Based Electronics")

SPRING MEETING
2018

Sant Feliu de Guíxols (Catalonia, Spain) 12-14 March 2018

Abstract Submission New Deadline 17th January 2018
PROGRAMME
SATELLITE EVENT

The COST TO-BE Spring Meeting 2018 will be organized by the ICMAB and will be held in Hotel Eden Roc, Sant Feliu, Spain 12-14th March 2018

This TO-BE meeting, being the last one of a series of Meetings organized by the TO-BE consortium, will be the stage for presentation of the contents of the Roadmap “Towards oxide electronics” that has been prepared within the context of TO-BE Cost Action. The different contributions to the Roadmap will be presented by the corresponding authors. The meeting will also offer the opportunity to present latest results by attendants. Participation at the meeting is free of charge and is open to scientists, both from the public research sector and from the private sector. Support for travel and subsistence will be offered to a limited numbers of participants. Requests for support should be made before the deadline, together with the abstract submission
