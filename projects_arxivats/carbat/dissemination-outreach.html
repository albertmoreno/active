Written on 27 February 2019. Posted in Outreach.

Prof. Johansson at Luleå University 2019

CARBAT WP leader, Prof. Patrik Johansson from Chalmers University, was invited at the Luleå University of Technology in Sweden on February 6th, 2019.

Luleå University of Technology is the northernmost university of technology in Scandinavia, offering world-class education and research in a variety of unique fields, including some of Sweden’s only master programmes in space engineering and spacecraft design. The research at Luleå University of Technology is conducted in close cooperation with companies such as Bosch, Ericsson, Scania, LKAB, SKF and leading international universities.

Prof. Johansson presented "Next Generation Batteries: Why & How?"
