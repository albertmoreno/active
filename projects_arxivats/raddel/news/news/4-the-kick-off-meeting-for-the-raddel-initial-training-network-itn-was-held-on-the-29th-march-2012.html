The kick-off meeting for the RADDEL Initial Training Network (ITN) was held on the 29th March 2012:

Details
Created on 13 April 2012

From left to right: Back row - Malcolm L. H. Green (External advisor); Khuloud Al-Jamal (KCL), Richard Zimmerman (IBA), Dimitris Emfietzoglou (UOI), Gerard Tobias (ICMAB-CSIC, Project coordinator), Jorge Pérez (ICMAB-CSIC, Project manager), Guillaume Bort (UOX), Martin Kalbáč (HIPC). Front row - Julie Wang (KCL), Hanene Ali-Boucetta (UCL), Cécilia Ménard-Moyon (CNRS), Tatiana Da Ros (UNITS).

RADDEL project, funded under the FP7 Marie Curie actions, deals with the development of nanocapsules for targeted delivery of radioactivity. The meeting represented the oppotunity for all the partners to meet each other and discuss the project in details. 12 people attended the kick-off meeting, with representatives of 9 of the 11 partners.
