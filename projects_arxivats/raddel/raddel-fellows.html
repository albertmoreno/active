Fellows participating in RADDEL

Details
Created on 13 April 2012

As an Initial Training Network (ITN) project, RADDEL offers Early-Stage (ESR) and Experienced Researchers (ER) the opportunity to participate in the project aiming to improve their personal and research skills, and to join established research teams to enhance their career prospects.

The updated list of all the fellows participating in this project will be available in this section.
